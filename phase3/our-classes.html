Classes Currently Offered!

Our classes are taught for FREE by our volunteer teachers.

Synchronous classes lasts 5 weeks (during school semester) or 5 days in one week (during school break), and meet for 1-1.5 hours a day once a week after school.
Classes are held on Zoom, and are available to students of all ages and experience levels.

Our Async-Material classes are ones which have class materials that you can download.
These materials include powerpoints, teacher's notes, and/or class recordings which the teacher used in previous sessions

Browse our class selection below, and signup!
